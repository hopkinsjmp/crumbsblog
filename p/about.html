About Crumbs of Sanity

Hello, I'm Carmel!

Welcome to Crumbs of Sanity, a cozy corner of the internet where academia meets the kitchen. As a PhD survivor, I found that cooking and baking became my sanctuary during the most challenging times of my academic journey.

Why "Crumbs of Sanity"?

The name came to me during a particularly stressful period of thesis writing. While everyone else was telling me to "keep it together," I found peace in letting things get a little messy – just like the inevitable crumbs that come with baking. These crumbs became my breadcrumb trail back to sanity.

What You'll Find Here

  • Comforting Recipes: From quick stress-relief snacks to celebratory feast-worthy dishes
  • Academic Life Stories: Because sometimes you need to know you're not alone
  • Kitchen Wisdom: Tips and tricks for busy scholars and anyone who needs a break
  • Mindful Moments: Finding peace in the process of creating something delicious

My Philosophy

I believe that cooking is more than just following a recipe – it's a form of self-care, a creative outlet, and sometimes, the perfect procrastination activity. Every recipe here comes with a story, because food is about more than just ingredients; it's about the moments we create and the comfort we find in the process.
