Types of Contributions We Love

  • Recipe Stories: Share a recipe that got you through a tough academic period

Submission Guidelines

Recipe Posts

Your recipe submission should include:

  • Recipe name and brief description
  • Your personal story connected to the recipe
  • Clear list of ingredients with measurements
  • Step-by-step instructions
  • High-quality photos (at least 3)
  • Prep time, cook time, and serving size
  • Any academic tie-ins or study break tips

Story Posts

For non-recipe contributions, please include:

  • A compelling title
  • Your academic background
  • Personal anecdotes and experiences
  • Practical tips or takeaways
  • Relevant photos (if applicable)

Technical Requirements

  • Text submissions should be 800-2000 words
  • Photos should be high-resolution (minimum 1200px wide)
  • Include a brief bio and headshot
  • Provide your social media handles (optional)

Brand Collaborations

For brands interested in partnering with Crumbs of Sanity, we offer:

  • Sponsored recipe development
  • Product reviews
  • Instagram collaborations
  • Newsletter features

How to Submit

Send your submissions or collaboration inquiries to:

Email: contribute@crumbsofsanity.com

Please allow 2-3 weeks for a response. We review all submissions carefully and will contact you if we feel your contribution is a good fit for our community.

Rights and Usage

By submitting content to Crumbs of Sanity, you:

  • Retain copyright of your work
  • Grant us permission to publish and promote your content
  • Agree to our standard contributor agreement (sent upon acceptance)

Questions?

If you have any questions about contributing, please don't hesitate to reach out. We're here to help you share your story with our community.
