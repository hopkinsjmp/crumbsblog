This Cookie Policy explains how *Crumbs of Sanity* ("we", "our", or "us") uses cookies and similar technologies on our blog [https://www.crumbsofsanity.com/](https://www.crumbsofsanity.com/).

By continuing to browse the site, you agree to our use of cookies as described in this policy, unless you choose to disable them via your browser or our cookie banner.

## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help us recognize your device, remember your preferences, and improve your browsing experience.

## 2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies:

**a. Strictly Necessary Cookies**

These cookies are essential for the website to function properly (e.g., page navigation, security, cookie consent). You cannot opt out of these.

**Examples:**

- Cookie that remembers your consent preferences
- Session cookies (if applicable)

**b. Performance/Analytics Cookies** *(Require consent)*

These cookies collect information about how visitors use our blog, such as which pages are most visited. They help us improve performance and user experience.

**Examples (if applicable):**

- Google Analytics

We only set these cookies **after you provide consent** via our cookie banner.

**c. Functionality Cookies** *(Optional)*

These cookies allow the blog to remember choices you make (e.g., name and email in comment forms) for a more personalized experience.

**d. Third-Party Cookies** *(Optional & consent-based)*

Some third-party services used on our blog may set their own cookies. These could include:

- Embedded YouTube or Vimeo videos
- Social sharing tools (e.g., Instagram feed, Facebook Like buttons)
- Comment platforms (e.g., Disqus)
- Substack (used to manage newsletter subscriptions)

Each of these third parties has its own cookie and privacy policies. For more information on Substack's practices, please visit [Substack's Privacy Policy](https://substack.com/privacy).

## 3. How to Manage Cookies

When you first visit our site, you will be asked to consent to non-essential cookies. You can:

- **Accept All** – We'll set all allowed cookies.
- **Reject Non-Essential** – Only essential cookies will be used.
- **Customize Preferences** – Choose what categories to allow.

You can also manage or delete cookies through your browser settings:

- **Chrome:** https://support.google.com/accounts/answer/32050
- **Firefox:** https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/clear-cookies-and-site-data-firefox
- **Safari:** https://support.apple.com/en-gb/guide/safari/sfri11471/mac
- **Edge:** https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/help/4027947
